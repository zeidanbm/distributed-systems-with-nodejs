Radar Chat

Leave Digital Messages in the Physical World.

Radar Chat is a mobile app (currently a PWA) that I've been building for the past several months and is currently in open beta. It's designed to help you find garage sales, food trucks, good coffee, etc. Sign up and let me know what you think!
